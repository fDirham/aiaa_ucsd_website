Our Organization

Our goal as a pre-professional student organization is to serve our members by providing an inclusive and supportive group that emphasizes mentorship, learning, and interconnectivity, and by preparing them for a future in aerospace. We aim to connect students to a national network of industry and academic professionals, hiring managers, and high-profile members of the aerospace community.
